Life-Saving Features

Every feature in Be Ready is designed with one goal: keeping you and your family safe when it matters most.

Instant SOS Access

Our prominent SOS button provides immediate access to emergency services. No fumbling through menus when time is critical.

Emergency Contacts

Access critical contacts instantly:

  • Pre-programmed emergency services (911, Poison Control)
  • Personal emergency contacts with one-tap calling
  • Medical provider information
  • Relationship identifiers for quick recognition

Emergency Locations & Navigation

Find help when you need it most:

  • Nearest hospitals and emergency services
  • Real-time directions to critical facilities
  • Distance and travel time displayed
  • One-tap calling to emergency facilities

Evacuation Planning

Be ready for any emergency:

  • Designated evacuation points and routes
  • Public shelters and emergency assembly areas
  • Personal meeting points for family coordination
  • Quick access to evacuation procedures

Critical Medical Information

Life-saving medical data at your fingertips:

  • Blood type, allergies, and medical conditions
  • Current medications and dosages
  • Emergency medical contacts
  • Treatment preferences and restrictions

Secure Document Storage

Important documents when you need them:

  • Insurance cards and policy information
  • Emergency identification documents
  • Quick access for first responders
  • Secure, offline storage for privacy

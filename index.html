Essential Wildfire Preparedness Features

Every feature in ResQLink is designed with one goal: keeping your family safe when a wildfire approaches.

Real-Time Wildfire Alerts

Stay ahead of the threat with instant notifications. Our app monitors official sources to alert you to nearby fire activity and evacuation warnings or orders for your designated zones.

Custom Evacuation Plans

Build and share a complete evacuation plan:

  • Map multiple evacuation routes
  • Set primary and secondary family meeting locations
  • Find nearby designated public shelters
  • Integrate with real-time traffic alerts

"Go-Bag" Checklists

Never forget a critical item. Use our pre-loaded or custom checklists for the "Six P's" and other essentials to prepare your emergency kits.

  • Checklists for family, pets, and vehicles
  • Track expiration dates for supplies
  • Set reminders to review and update your kits

Secure Document & Info Hub

Access vital information even when you're offline. Securely store digital copies of:

  • Insurance policies and identification
  • Emergency contacts and medical data
  • Photos of home and valuables for claims

Home Hardening Guides

Prepare your home to better withstand a wildfire. Our guides help you:

  • Follow checklists to create defensible space
  • Learn about fire-resistant landscaping
  • Get tips for reducing wildfire risks
  • Set reminders for seasonal maintenance

Family Communication Plan

Ensure everyone is on the same page during a crisis:

  • Designate an out-of-state contact
  • Share the evacuation plan instantly
  • Use the "I'm Safe" status check button
  • Store emergency contacts for one-tap calls

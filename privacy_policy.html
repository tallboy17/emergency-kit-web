Privacy Policy for ResQLink

Effective Date: September 21, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

ResQLink ("the App") is designed with your privacy and security as the highest priority. We understand that the information you store in the app is sensitive and personal. This policy outlines our firm stance on data privacy: we collect no information from you.

The Core Principle: All information you add to the ResQLink app is stored exclusively on your local device. Nothing is ever transmitted, saved, or viewed by us or any third party.

Information You Provide

The App allows you to input and store critical information to help you prepare for a wildfire evacuation. This may include, but is not limited to:

  • Emergency Contacts
  • Evacuation Routes and Meeting Points
  • "Go-Bag" Checklists
  • Medical Information
  • Copies of Personal Documents
  • Home and Property Details

This data resides solely within the secure storage of your phone. Deleting the app will also delete all the data you have entered.

Information We Do NOT Collect

To be perfectly clear, ResQLink does NOT collect, store, or have access to any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any other personal identifiers.
  • Your location data or GPS coordinates.
  • Usage analytics or crash reports.
  • Any of the personal data you enter into the app.

There are no user accounts to create because we have no user data to store. The app functions entirely offline without needing an internet connection to access your stored plans.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. Any changes will be reflected in an updated version of the app and a revised policy. We will never change our core principle of not collecting your personal data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us at support@resqlink-app.com.
